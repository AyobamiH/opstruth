result language

The output is the product surface.

Verified

Facts the command actually checked.

repo, quality, exact inputs

Warnings

Risk signals with evidence and next steps.

review before shipping

Failures

Checks that ran and did not pass.

not hidden as noise

Not Verified

Claims that still need proof.

unknown is not safe
Verified, warning, failure, skipped, and not verified are separate states.
evidence per finding

A warning has to show its work.

Findings include file, line, pattern class, operational meaning, and a next safe step without printing raw secrets.
